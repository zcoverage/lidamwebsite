Property & Casualty FAQs

Auto Insurance

What does auto insurance cover?

Auto insurance can cover damage to your vehicle, injuries, and liability for accidents you cause. Most policies include liability, collision, and comprehensive coverage options.

Do I need full coverage?

If your vehicle is financed or leased, full coverage is usually required. Otherwise, it depends on your car’s value and your comfort level with potential repair costs.

What is SR-22 insurance?

An SR-22 is a form filed with your state proving you carry the required liability coverage, often needed after license suspension or certain violations.

Homeowners & Renters

What does homeowners insurance protect against?

Home insurance protects your property and belongings from damage caused by fire, storms, theft, and more. It can also include liability coverage if someone is injured on your property.

Is renters insurance worth it?

Yes — renters insurance is affordable and protects your personal belongings and liability, even if you don’t own the property.

Landlord & Property Coverage

How is landlord insurance different from homeowners insurance?

Landlord insurance covers rental properties and includes protection for the structure, lost rental income, and liability. It typically excludes tenants’ personal belongings.

Do I need coverage for short-term rentals?

Yes. Short-term rental properties (like Airbnb) often require special endorsements or commercial policies to stay properly covered.

Business & Commercial Insurance

What types of business insurance do you offer?

We provide coverage for small businesses, contractors, and commercial vehicles — including general liability, workers’ comp, and business owner’s policies (BOP).

Is commercial auto insurance required?

If you use vehicles for business purposes, a commercial policy is required — even for personal vehicles used for work activities.

Discounts & Bundles

Can I bundle home and auto insurance?

Yes! Bundling home and auto often provides significant savings and simplifies your policy management.

What other discounts are available?

Discounts can include safe driver, multi-vehicle, paperless billing, security systems, and loyalty savings — depending on the carrier.

Claims & Support

How do I file a claim?

You can file a claim directly with your insurance carrier or contact us — we’ll help you through every step of the process.

How long does a claim take to process?

It varies by claim type and carrier. Minor auto claims may be settled within days; property claims can take longer depending on inspections and estimates.
